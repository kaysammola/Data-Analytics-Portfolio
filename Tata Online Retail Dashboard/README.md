# Tata Online Retail Dashboard

## Project Overview

This dashboard analyzes online retail transactions to uncover customer purchasing behavior, revenue generation, product demand, and geographic sales distribution.

---

## Business Problem

Retail organizations require data-driven insights to improve customer experience, optimize inventory, and maximize sales performance.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset includes:

- Orders
- Customers
- Products
- Revenue
- Countries
- Monthly Sales
- Product Quantities

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Data Modeling

---

## Dashboard Features

- Revenue KPI
- Orders KPI
- Customer KPI
- Monthly Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Country
- Top Selling Products
- Most Demanded Products
- Customer Performance

---

## Key Insights

- Generated approximately $10M in revenue.
- Processed over 26K customer orders.
- United Kingdom generated the highest revenue.
- November recorded the highest monthly sales.
- DOTCOM POSTAGE generated the highest revenue.
- Certain products experienced significantly higher customer demand.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Expand marketing activities in high-performing countries.
- Maintain inventory for best-selling products.
- Launch seasonal campaigns during peak months.
- Focus loyalty programs on repeat customers.
- Improve product recommendations using customer purchasing history.
