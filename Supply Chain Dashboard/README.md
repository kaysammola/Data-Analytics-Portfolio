# Supply Chain Dashboard

## Project Overview

This dashboard analyzes supply chain performance by evaluating revenue, supplier performance, shipping costs, manufacturing costs, customer demographics, and SKU performance.

---

## Business Problem

Supply chain managers require real-time insights to improve operational efficiency, reduce logistics costs, and optimize supplier performance.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset includes:

- Suppliers
- Products
- Revenue
- Shipping Costs
- Manufacturing Costs
- Customer Demographics
- SKUs

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX

---

## Dashboard Features

- Revenue KPI
- Manufacturing Cost KPI
- Shipping Cost KPI
- Supplier Analysis
- Carrier Performance
- SKU Performance
- Product Revenue Analysis

---

## Key Insights

- Total revenue exceeded $578K.
- Skincare products generated the highest revenue.
- Supplier 2 delivered the strongest overall performance.
- Carrier costs varied significantly.
- Customer demographics influenced purchasing behavior.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Strengthen relationships with high-performing suppliers.
- Optimize shipping carrier selection.
- Improve inventory planning for top-performing SKUs.
- Monitor manufacturing costs regularly.
- Enhance customer segmentation strategies.
