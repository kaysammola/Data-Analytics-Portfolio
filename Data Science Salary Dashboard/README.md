# Data Science Salary Dashboard

## Project Overview

This dashboard explores salary trends within the data science industry by job role, experience level, company size, employment type, and location.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations and professionals require salary insights to make informed hiring, compensation, and career decisions.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains:

- Job Titles
- Salaries
- Company Size
- Experience Level
- Employment Type
- Country
- Work Year

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX

---

## Dashboard Features

- Salary KPIs
- Salary by Experience
- Highest Paying Roles
- Salary by Country
- Salary Trend
- Company Size Analysis
- Employment Type Analysis

---

## Key Insights

- Average salary reached approximately $112K.
- Executive positions earned the highest salaries.
- Data Science Manager ranked as the highest-paid role.
- Large organizations offered the highest average salaries.
- Salaries increased consistently over time.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Benchmark salaries against industry standards.
- Invest in employee career development.
- Offer competitive compensation packages.
- Review salary structures annually.
- Expand recruitment in high-demand specialties.
