# 🎓 Student Mental Health Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Student Mental Health Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze the mental health status of university students. The dashboard provides valuable insights into depression, anxiety, treatment status, academic performance (CGPA), gender distribution, study year trends, and marital status. The objective is to help educational institutions better understand students' mental well-being and support data-driven decisions for improving mental health services.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Mental health has become a significant concern among university students, affecting academic performance, productivity, and overall well-being. Educational institutions require a centralized reporting solution to identify mental health patterns, monitor high-risk groups, and allocate support resources effectively.

This dashboard addresses these challenges by transforming raw student survey data into meaningful visual insights that support informed decision-making.

## 💡 Key Insights

- The dataset contains 101 student records.
- Approximately one-third of students reported experiencing depression.
- Anxiety levels were also reported by a significant proportion of students.
- Female students reported considerably higher levels of depression than male students.
- Anxiety was highest among first-year students and gradually declined in higher study years.
- Engineering students recorded the highest number of depression cases among academic programs.
- Married students showed higher depression levels, while single students reported higher anxiety levels.
- Most students experiencing depression and anxiety maintained CGPAs between 3.00–3.49 and 3.50–4.00, indicating that mental health challenges affect students across different academic performance levels.
