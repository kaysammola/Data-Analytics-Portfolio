# Retail Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides an interactive analysis of retail sales performance across different product categories, customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and seasonal trends. The dashboard enables business stakeholders to monitor sales performance, identify high-performing products, and make informed business decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Retail companies need clear insights into customer purchasing patterns, product performance, and seasonal sales trends to improve profitability and optimize inventory management.

This dashboard answers key business questions such as:

- Which product categories generate the highest sales?
- Which customer groups contribute the most revenue?
- How does seasonality affect sales?
- Which purchase sizes generate the most revenue?

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains retail transaction records including:

- Customer Demographics
- Product Categories
- Sales Amount
- Quantity Sold
- Purchase Size
- Gender

- Seasonal Sales

## Key Insights

- Generated approximately $456K in total sales.
- Electronics produced the highest sales revenue.
- Customers aged 31–50 contributed the highest sales.
- Four-item purchases generated the highest revenue.
- Female and male customers contributed almost equally to total sales.
- Spring recorded the strongest seasonal sales.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for top-performing product categories.
- Target marketing campaigns toward high-value customer segments.
- Introduce promotional bundles encouraging larger purchases.
- Prepare inventory ahead of peak sales seasons.
- Continue customer segmentation to improve marketing effectiveness.
